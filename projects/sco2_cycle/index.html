Supercritical co2 power cycle

Experiments and modelling to better understand a better alternative to the steam cycle.

The sCO\(_2\) cycle is in many ways a better alternative to the steam cycle. In my PhD I designed and performed experiments on sCO\(_2\) heat transfer in simulated cooling tower conditions, as well as developed detailed heat exchanger and cycle off-design numerical predictive models.

The supercritical fluid loop at the University of Southern Queensland

Using dimensional analysis and LASSO sparse regression to develop new heat transfer correlations. This proved highly effective for understanding which dimensionless numbers, out of hundreds of potential candidates, best describe the data.
